Reservations

Kimball Hall has three common spaces that are availble for reservations by student groups: the main lounge, the side lounge, and the recording studio. Reservations for all groups are limited to a maximum 2 hour reservation on Mondays - Thursdays from 4pm - 10pm each week, and can only be made one week in advance.

All groups using any of Kimball Hall's common spaces agree to clean up after themselves and to respect the residents and the property of the dorm.

To request an account on the reservation system for your student group, please email the Kimball RCCs at kimball-rcc@rescomp.stanford.edu. Please allow 24 hours for the RCCs to process the reservation account.

All rules and regulations must be adhered to. Student groups found in violation of these terms will be have their reservation privileges revoked.
