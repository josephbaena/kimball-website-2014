Preassign Policy

As pre-assigns, you received first draw for rooms, and will have first priority for most classes, workshops and other arts related activities in the dorm. In turn, you must earn at least 5 pre-assign points each quarter by organizing or participating in themed activities to help energize the arts and the wider community here in Kimball.

Here are a few ways you can get more deeply involved in the arts and earn pre-assign points:

  • Take in-house creative classes for credit (e.g. StoryCraft) - 2 points
  • Teach a creative workshop series (a workshop series is a quarter-long workshop meeting in Kimbal at least once per week) - 2 points
  • Lead a quarterly arts group (e.g. a writer's group, a cooking club, or an in-house quartet) - 2 points
  • Collaborate with one or more pre-assigns or residents across genres in a quarter-long creative project - there are grants to help with funding! - 2 points
  • Assist ATAs with ongoing arts duties in-house (art studio, gallery, recording studio, etc.) - 1 or 2 points
  • Take a workshop series from a staff member, fellow pre-assign, or resident - 1 point
  • Attend a quarterly arts group - 1 point
  • Teach a single creative workshop (as opposed to a series, single workshops meet only once) - 1 point
  • Attend a single creative workshop - 1 point
  • Arrange and lead an arts event on or off campus - 1 point
  • Attend an arts event on or off campus - 1 point
  • Perform or show creative work at Kimball (including open mic) - 1 point

    • Most of the activities above fall into at least one of four categories:

    Learn. Teach. Create. Collaborate

    We encourage you to seek activities in Kimball that will help you grow in one or more of these ways throughout the year.

    If you wish to host an event, reach out to an ATA to figure out an appropriate number of points to allocate to that event, and they will create the event for you!



    Point System Overview

    Whenever you attend a Kimball event that grants preassign points you need to go to the Kimball Preassign page and enter the "Preassign Portal".

    Once you've logged in, you will see a dashboard which tells you how many points you've earned, and a list of preassign events

    The right-most column is used to mark event participation. Check off the events you attended/hosted and click "Submit Attendance".


    The row for the events you checked off will change to red and show "pending approval".


    The ATAs will then receive the attendance request and they will verify that you attended or hosted the event and grant the points. Once the attendance is approved, the row for that event will change to green and show "Approved" and your point total will be updated.
