Live in the Arts.

Nestled in the quaint Manzanita Park at Stanford University, Kimball Hall houses over 200 students, a large lounge with 2 grand pianos and a professional lighting system, a small lounge with an upright piano and an HD television with cable, 2 study rooms, 3 alcoves, and a recording studio. Every year the Kimball staff organizes opportunities for residents to engage with the visual and performing arts, both on and off campus.
